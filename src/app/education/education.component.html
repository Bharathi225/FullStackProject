Primary School

Primary education is the first formal stage of education covering 5-6 years of academic study starts with classes 6th-10th, We can say that it forms the cornerstone of academic careers. Primary education providers must impart a secure and positive environment where effective learning occurs.

Secondary School

The secondary stage of education covering 2-3 years of academic study starts with classes 11th-12th, consisting of students aged between 14-16 years. The schools that impart education up to 12th class are known as Secondary Schools, High Schools, Senior Schools, etc.
